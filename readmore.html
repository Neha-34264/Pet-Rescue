Adoption Guide

Pet adoption requirements vary depending on where you’re adopting a cat or dog from. Some rescues and shelters have more strict requirements to adopt a pet than others, so it’s important to know what is needed of you before you start the adoption process. Typically, shelters and rescues have more requirements to adopt a cat or dog than a breeder, but it depends on each organization.

If you want to adopt a pet through PetRehomer, you must…

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Prove your identity with photo ID such as a driving license or passport
  • Prove your home address with a utility bill, mortgage statement or similar official document from the past three months
  • Get permission from your landlord or check the terms of your lease before you start the adoption process to make sure you’re allowed a pet
  • Agree to having a home check
  • Agree to buy your pet appropriate food, equipment and shelter
  • Agree to continue with any training or behavioural rehabilitation
  • Agree to pay for veterinary care, regular flea and worm treatments, and neutering if the pet is old enough
  • Agree to care for your pet’s mental and physical health at all times
  • Seriously consider taking out pet insurance. We strongly encourage everyone to get their pets insured

Frequently Asked Questions For Adoption

How can I adopt a pet?

Adopting a pet is a very important decision that leads to an even more rewarding experience. Before adopting, make sure you are able to take on the responsibilities associated with pet ownership and are able to provide quality love and care for your new companion.

What are the adoption fees?

The adoption fees vary depending on the age, breed, and health condition of the pet. Please contact us or visit our website for more information about adoption fees.

Where can I go to adopt a pet?

You can adopt a pet from local animal shelters, rescue organizations, or animal welfare societies. Many pet stores also partner with shelters for adoption events. It's recommended to visit the facility in person or check their website for available pets and adoption process.

Do you offer adoption discounts?

Yes, we offer adoption discounts during special events or promotions. Check our website or visit our facility for current offers and discounts.

Do you allow shelter home dog introductions?

Yes, we encourage shelter home dog introductions to ensure compatibility with existing pets and family members. Our trained staff will guide you through the process and provide assistance as needed.
